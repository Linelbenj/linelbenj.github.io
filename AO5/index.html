Welcome to Amsterdam

City of Canals, Culture & Cozy Corners

Introduction

Amsterdam is the capital of the Netherlands, famous for its artistic heritage, canal system, and historic charm. The city blends culture, creativity, and coziness in every street and corner.

The beautiful canals offer a serene view of the city’s architecture and are a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Rijksmuseum is home to masterpieces by Rembrandt, Vermeer, and Van Gogh.

Things to Do

  • Bike around the city like a local
  • Take a canal cruise
  • Visit Anne Frank House
  • Explore the Jordaan neighborhood
  • Visit tulips garden

Spring time brings colorful tulips across Amsterdam — a floral wonderland!

Travel Tips

Best time to visit is April to May (tulip season) or September for mild weather and fewer tourists. Don’t forget a raincoat!
